Linguistic Diversity in Australia An exploration into the different languages Autralian's speak at home through the 2021 Australian Census

English Proficiency and Patterns

Who speaks English in Australia?

While Australia does not have an official language, English is the unofficial recognised language of Australia.

In the 2021 Australia Census, the variable ENGLP (Proficiency in Spoken English) was recorded for individuals who reported speaking a language other than English at home.
